// Commuter Tips
01
Leave Before 7AM
Beat the morning rush on Federal Highway and NPE by departing before 7AM. Traffic spikes sharply after 7:30AM.
02
Use LDP for Midday
Between 9AM–4PM, LDP is your best bet between Subang Jaya and PJ. Avoid Federal Highway during those windows.
03
Avoid SS15 at Lunch
The SS15 Subang Parade area gets congested between 12–2PM due to lunch crowds. Add 15–20 minutes buffer.
04
Friday PM is Worst
Friday evenings from 5PM onwards are the worst of the week. Consider WFH or delay departure until after 8:30PM.
